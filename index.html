CHROMOGIC

Chromogic is a color-logic puzzle game built around a simple rule: every row and column must contain exactly the right number of each color. Clue cells guide you toward the one unique solution hiding in each grid.

Start small with a 4×4 grid and two colors. Work your way up to the full 8×8 challenge with four colors and expert-level clues. In Marathon mode, the puzzles never stop — only your time does.
